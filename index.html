Bitcoin-inspired, people-focused. An independent experiment in hard money design, fairer distribution, and defence against heavy market manipulation.

Design Pillars

Fair(er) Distribution

Long-term cycles instead of a single sale. Real users and coordinated groups matter more than pure capital and multi-wallet abuse.

Time & Scarcity

Strict supply cap, non-inflationary logic, and slow release over many periods so BTJ can be measured in years, not one hype season.

Behavioural Security

Designed with whales, bears, and manipulation in mind – trying to make obvious abuse harder without freezing out serious participants.

Why “Bitcoin Junior”?

Bitcoin Junior is not here to replace Bitcoin. It is meant to be a next-generation, Bitcoin-inspired system built on the same core spirit:

  • Money without governments and central banks.
  • Rules instead of rulers.
  • Time, scarcity, and open consensus instead of promises.

BTJ also tries to be more tuned for today’s world: faster on innovation, more aware of behavioural risks, and structurally anti-inflationary in how supply and distribution work.

Roadmap – Very Early View

  1. Design & Discussion: iterate on the whitepaper, tokenomics, and distribution logic with public feedback.
  2. Prototype & Launch: deploy the initial BTJ token on a host chain and start early distribution cycles.
  3. Build the Ecosystem: non-custodial wallet, dashboards, and community tools – and only later, if justified, explore a dedicated BTJ chain or rollup.
